FERARRI F8 Spider

MALIBU, California—First revealed last September, the new 2020 Ferrari F8 Spider picks up where Ferrari left off with the 488 Spider. Automobile contributor Arthur St. Antoine once wrote, "There's a strong case to be made that the Ferrari 488 Spider is the single greatest modern automobile for sale in all the world." Well, Art, you were right—until now, as we learned during our recent first test drive of Maranello's latest mid-engine V-8 thriller.
